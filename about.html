About

Exrio, officially the Sultanate of Exrio (Arabic: السلطنة الإكسروانية, Exrian: Sultankara ni Eksria) is an Islamic micronation approximated to have been founded between the years 2010 and 2012 by Mahmoud bin Umar, in the land of Australia, albeit it was not governed at this time. On 10 October 2015, it became a republic for some time before it returned to its non-governing state. On 9 June 2022, Exrio became a Sultanate adopting Salafism and Atharism as its basis for the law and theological practices.
